Vital Kauaʻi · A Private Church Association

Membership Application & Agreement, Read in full before signing

By becoming a member of Vital Kauaʻi, I agree to the following terms and conditions:

1Vital Kauaʻi's purpose is furthering the awakening, transformation, and vitality of people throughout the world, the betterment of life on planet Earth, and other spiritual, humanitarian, and beneficent purposes.
2Vital Kauaʻi is a creation of free men and women to associate with each other in the private domain according to their church and talents.
3Vital Kauaʻi is a platform on which members may, for Vital Kauaʻi's good purpose, conduct all manner of private speech and business with Vital Kauaʻi, members, and other associations – keeping all business in the private domain and utilizing the protections thereof.
4Vital Kauaʻi is a private, unincorporated Church that operates outside the jurisdiction of government entities, agencies, officers, agents, contractors, and other representatives, as protected by law.
5Vital Kauaʻi lawfully stands on the authority our Creator gives to free men and women as revealed in the holy Scriptures, the Constitution of the United States of America, and the constitutions of the several states of the union.
6Members claim the right to freedom of religion, free speech, petition, assembly, and the right to gather in association to assert our rights protected by those Constitutions, Charter, and Statutes.
7Members claim the right to be free from unreasonable search and seizure, the right to not incriminate ourselves, and the right to freely exercise all other unalienable rights as granted by our creator, our almighty God, and guaranteed by those Constitutions, Charter, and Statutes.
8Members decide for themselves which member(s) qualify to provide them counsel and advice concerning all matters, including but not limited to physical, mental, emotional, spiritual, healthcare, law, and any other matter, and may contract for counsel, advice, services, etc. they believe assist their ministry through Vital Kauaʻi.
9Members have the freedom to choose and perform for ourselves the types of therapies and treatments we think best for diagnosing, treating, and preventing illness and disease and for achieving and maintaining optimum wellness, as well as the freedom to choose any types of assistance in lawful matters and any other private business activity.
10Vital Kauaʻi will recognize as a member any person(s), natural or otherwise (irrespective of race, color, or religion) who has joined Vital Kauaʻi or its social media groups and has agreed to the terms of membership, providing said person has not been sanctioned, exercised, or otherwise banned by Vital Kauaʻi.
11Vital Kauaʻi's stewards, or their designee, may at any time terminate my membership should they conclude I am interacting with them or other members in a way that is contrary or detrimental to the focus, principles, and betterment of Vital Kauaʻi.
12Vital Kauaʻi is protected by the First and Fourteenth Amendments to the U.S. Constitution and outside the jurisdiction and authority of Federal and State Agencies and Authorities concerning complaints or grievances against Vital Kauaʻi, members, or other staff persons. All rights of complaints or grievances will be settled by a Vital Kauaʻi designee, committee, or tribunal and will be waived by the member for the benefit of Vital Kauaʻi and its members.
13Vital Kauaʻi and member activity is under common law rather than statutory law or regulatory law, which are creations of public government for the public.
14In my relations as a member, I voluntarily change my capacity from that of a public person to that of a private member.
15My activities within Vital Kauaʻi are matters of private contracts that I refuse to share with Local, State, or Federal investigative or enforcement agencies. I agree not to pursue legal action against a fellow member of Vital Kauaʻi unless that member has exposed me to a clear and present danger of substantive harm and upon the recommendation and approval of Vital Kauaʻi.
16I do not, and will not while a member, represent any Local, State or Federal agency whose purpose is to regulate and approve products or services or to conduct any mission of enforcement, entrapment, or investigation.
17I may, by written notice to Vital Kauaʻi, withdraw from this agreement and terminate membership at any time. I will not misrepresent myself as being a member beyond the term of my membership.
18Vital Kauaʻi may revoke my membership if I engage in abusive, violent, menacing, destructive or harassing behavior towards any other member of Vital Kauaʻi.
19These pages consist of the entire agreement for membership in Vital Kauaʻi.
20I enter into this agreement freely, without duress or coercion.
21I hereby exercise my right of "freedom of association" as guaranteed by the Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR), the U.S. Constitution, and equivalent provisions of the various State Constitutions.
22I agree this contract began on the date of my joining Vital Kauaʻi. I declare that by joining Vital Kauaʻi and/or Vital Kauaʻi's websites and/or social media group(s), I have carefully read this document and I understand and agree with it.
