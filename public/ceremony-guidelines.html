Ceremony Guidelines

These guidelines exist to support your deepest transformation. They are a living invitation, held with care by your facilitators and by each person who enters this space.

◇

Sacred Agreements

Entering this container is an act of sacred commitment, to yourself, to those sharing the space with you, and to the work we are here to do together. Please hold these agreements with the same reverence you bring to your healing.

  • I align with my highest positive intention for the good of all.
  • I arrive on time, having eaten lightly and remained free of alcohol and substances.
  • I honor and protect the confidentiality of this group.
  • I respect all practices, protocols, and teachings as proprietary to Vital Kauaʻi and hold them within this container.
  • I take full responsibility for my own experience, using "I" statements and owning my own process.
  • I ask before offering support, making sure those around me are seeking help before extending it.
  • I travel home only when I feel fully grounded and safe to do so.
  • I honor the purpose of this gathering and the trust it requires.
◯

Confidentiality & the Sacred Container

Everything shared and experienced within this space is sacred. We ask that you hold complete confidentiality, not only the identities of those present, but the details of their experiences, processes, and private shares.

You are welcome to speak about your own personal feelings and insights. We ask that you keep the specific practices and exercises within this container, as they can be misunderstood without the full context in which they are held.

  • When sharing with a partner or loved one, focus on your own experience rather than what others shared or did in the space.
  • Within any group container, do not assume you may share another participant's experience with someone else from the same group.
  • Photos, video, and social media shares are not appropriate within ceremony or session spaces.
♡

Caring for Yourself

Your wellbeing is the foundation of the work. You are the most important variable in your own healing, and tending to yourself with care creates the conditions for genuine transformation.

  • Drink water, use the restroom freely, move your body, and have a snack as needed throughout our time together.
  • Stay attuned to your comfort, physical, emotional, and energetic.
  • Know your yes's and your no's, and trust them. Everything offered is an invitation, not a requirement.
  • Notice the protective voice in your mind, the impulse toward fight, flight, freeze, numbing, or dissociation, and meet it with curiosity and breath.
  • When in doubt, do less. Less is always honored here.
  • When difficult material arises, return to breath, sound, and movement as your anchors.
  • Give your full 100%, understanding that everyone's 100% looks beautifully different.
✦

Presence Within the Space

Ceremony and deep work call for a quality of awareness, a sustained turning inward, even when held in the company of others.

  • Alone Together: Imagine a soft energy bubble around you. Stay anchored in your own process, allowing what is happening in the larger field to exist without pulling your attention away from yourself.
  • Body Wisdom: Relax and be present. Your body holds intelligence that the thinking mind cannot access. Trust that intelligence, and trust the constellation of this space.
  • Heart Guardians: You are always held and protected within this container. Surrender is safe here.
  • Everything is a suggestion: The invitations and guidance we offer are exactly that. Your body's wisdom always takes precedence. Follow what feels true.
  • Trauma and activation: When your body activates, breath, sound, and gentle movement are your guides back to presence. You do not need to push through, you need only to return.
  • Save questions: Hold questions until the designated Q&A time so the space stays open and uninterrupted for everyone.
  • Gentleness: Be gentle with yourself and with one another. This is the most important thing.
◈

Facilitator Presence & Boundaries

Your facilitators hold this space as mirrors, guides, and fellow travelers, never as authority over your experience. Understanding how we show up supports everyone in receiving the most from this work.

  • Healer as Mirror: Your facilitators are human. When personal material arises in us, we name it internally and return our full presence to you. Our healing and yours are always separate rivers, even when they run alongside each other.
  • Empathy, not Sympathy: We meet you in your experience rather than absorbing it or fixing it for you. This distinction keeps the work clean and powerful.
  • Love, not Judgment: There is nothing you can bring into this space that will be met with anything other than compassionate presence.
  • Trust, not Control: We hold structure and safety, and within that, we release the need to control your process. Your journey unfolds exactly as it is meant to.
  • Ask before offering: Facilitators will always check in before extending physical support or energy work, honoring your autonomy at every step.

Be on purpose. Align with your highest intention for the good of all, and trust that this space, these guides, and the wisdom of your own body will carry you exactly where you need to go.
