Section 6

Health & Safety Disclosure

Complete and honest disclosure here is essential — for your safety and for the integrity of the work. Everything you share will be received with care, and nothing will end the conversation before it begins.

Iboga is a powerful medicine with specific cardiac and drug interaction considerations. The more complete your disclosure here, the more precisely we can prepare with you and, where needed, coordinate with your physician before arrival.

Include physical, mental, and neurological. Include anything currently managed or historical.

Include dose and frequency. SSRIs, MAOIs, antipsychotics, and psychiatric medications are particularly important to disclose for iboga work.

Heart conditions, prolonged QT interval, liver concerns, history of psychosis, recent substance use — please be thorough.
